<p style="text-align: justify;">We are the Intelligent System (IntelliSys) Lab at <a href="https://www.stevens.edu/school-engineering-science/departments/electrical-computer-engineering">the ECE Department</a> of <a href="https://www.stevens.edu">Stevens Institute of Technology</a>. Our research focuses on bridging artificial intelligence (AI) and emerging system architectures by developing efficient, robust algorithms and advancing intelligent, high-performance computing systems. Supported by multiple NSF grants, as well as funding and credits from AWS, NIH AIM-AHEAD, and the Computing Research Association (CRA), our work spans theory, algorithms, and real-world systems. Explore our <a href="https://intellisys.haow.us/projects/">projects</a>. </p>

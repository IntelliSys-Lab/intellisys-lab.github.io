### DuckAI: The iCNS/ECE Symposium on AI Research and Innovations 

The iCNS/ECE Symposium on AI Research and Innovations (DuckAI) is a series of events organized by the Center for Innovative Computing and Networked Systems (iCNS) and the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Stevens Institute of Technology. These events bring together industry partners, researchers, faculty, and students to discuss recent developments in AI techniques and applications.
